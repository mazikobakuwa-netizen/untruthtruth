The Tariff War

In Early 2025, The United States imposed tariffs on their allies: The EU, Canada, and Mexico. This examines multiple domestic and foreign sources to determine which major player's economic pain is considered front-page worthy.

Wall Street Journal:How Uncertainty From Trump’s Tariffs Is Rippling Through the Economy

Source Link
Details how Canada, the EU, and Mexico are experiencing the effects of tarrifs imposed by the current administration, while highlighting the hardship effect on companies and consumers. Describes how the tariffs will affect the cost of goods and shipping, as well as timing, by increasing costs on consumers to accomodate the tariff's financial blowback.
Bias:Low Favors: The EU, Canada, and Mexico

Global Times China:"U.S. Trade Aggression Threatens Multilateral Order"

Source Link
Explores the Chinese approach to reciprocal tariffs imposed by the current administration, communicating that China's stance is collaborative while the U.S.'s stance is disruptive. Uses language like "hegomanic behavior," "myopic and irresponsible," "chaotic and unpredictable" in reference to the United States actions and behaior.
Bias: High Favors: China
